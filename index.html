Here, you'll find a showcase of my recent web development work, highlighting my commitment to creating websites that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

BookShelf+

Bookshelf+ is a user-friendly website that simplifies book collection organization. You can effortlessly add new books and sort them into two shelves: "To Be Read" and "Completed," making it easy to track your reading progress and move books between shelves as needed. Deleting unnecessary books is straightforward. Importantly, Bookshelf+ secures your book data with localStorage, even when the webpage is closed. Developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this site is one of my projects completed during a Dicoding course.

Sistem Informasi Futsal

The Futsal Information System website is a modern solution for futsal field rental businesses. Built using HTML, CSS, jQuery, and PHP technologies, this system simplifies the management of field bookings and schedules. Customers can also book fields online without the need to visit in person. With secure data storage features, this system aims to enhance operational efficiency and provide a better experience in the futsal field rental business. On this website, I played a dual role as both the frontend and backend developer.

MyNote+

Mynote is a web application built using cutting-edge technologies, including Tailwind CSS and ReactJS, designed to help you organize your notes effortlessly. With a sleek and intuitive interface, Mynote provides a user-friendly experience for creating, editing, deleting, and archiving notes. Whether you're jotting down important information, drafting ideas, or simply keeping track of your thoughts, Mynote offers a seamless platform to manage your notes with ease. Experience the power of modern web development with Mynote and stay organized like never before.
